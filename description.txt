Wilhelmines Texture Pack

A new Texture Pack for the lovers of natural colors and landscapes.

(Partially) supported mods:

-PaleoTest
-Water Life
- Xocean
-Farming
-Nether

Have FUN! :D

Installation: Place the folder (Wilhelmines Texture Pack) in your minetest/textures/ folder and choose the Texture Pack ingame in the Content Menu.
